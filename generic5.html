Relative Elevation Model -Gooimeer,Netherlands

The Map above is a relative elevation model. I chose the Goimeer as it is man-made and I thought it would be interesting to see what kind of map could be rendered. The Goimeer is a large river it is interestin to investigate rivers like these as flood control infrastructure always makes for an interesting map!

To create the map above, I selected three tiles on Geotiles of the area. I merged these layers and created a hillshade to define my map. I drew the crosslines along the Goimeer and then ran a TIN Interpolation. Unfortunatley the TIN Interpolation didn''t render.

I chose the blue colour scheme above because I thought it was fitting with the main focus being a water feature., however we are free to choose whatever colours we like!
