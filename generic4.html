Vector Data

The Map above demonstrates Vector data that consists of alert levels for coral reefs worldwide. Vector data is a type of spatial data used in GIS to represent features. It is made up of geometric shapes such as points,lines and polygons.

The Data presented in the map above is from the NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration) Coral Reef Watch, this data is from satellite based measurements of ocean temperature. The data consists of alert levels for the coral reef, as you can see on the map the scale is based on the likelyhood of coral bleaching: 0 meaning no heat stress to 4 meaning coral mortality likely

This map is interactive!! Go ahead and tap on any point to get details!!
